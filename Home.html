Welcome to EDM Qluster

EDM Qluster welcomes you to one of the greatest online communities you'll ever come across. Plug in and meet our friendly staff members along with our regular enthusiastic users.

Music

Our community is known for the variety of EDM genres constantly being played day and night. You can join in with the fun by sharing your favourite EDM tracks or you can just sit back, relax and listen to other people's songs.

Qbot

We run a lightweight moderation bot called Qbot that watches over the room, helps moderators do their job and also comes with a few fun commands users can use. Qbot is also very friendly and greets everyone who joins.

Events

Our community also hosts themed events which creates a way for up and coming EDM artists to get recognised and socialise with their fans. You can find info on any upcoming events above so you don't miss out.

Who we are

EDM Qluster is an up-and-coming plug.dj community. We are a devoted team and users alike whose goal is to provide a great atmosphere, full of enthusiastic people sharing their love for electronic music. Our community is always active with users from around the world.
